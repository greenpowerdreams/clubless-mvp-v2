# DJ & Promoter Outreach — DM Templates

**Goal:** Recruit 20-30 Seattle DJs and promoters as early Clubless users + link exchanges (their Linktree → clublesscollective.com + maybe a reciprocal on `/creators` or a vendor profile).

**Channel:** Instagram DM is most effective for Seattle scene. Secondary: email if publicly listed.

**Rule:** Personalize the first line or don't bother. Scene people can smell a mass blast in 2 seconds.

---

## DM v1 — "I've been to your events" opener (highest conversion)

Yo [name] — hope you're good. I was at [specific event / set] and wanted to say that was [specific thing that landed].

I'm Drew — I run Clubless Collective, a Seattle platform I just launched for creators who throw events without owning a venue. It's ticketing (8-10% fee, lower than Posh), a licensed mobile bar we run ourselves, and a profit calculator so you can model a night before you commit.

I'm quietly onboarding the first 20 Seattle creators who I think should be on it before we open it wide. Would love to set you up free — you'd be the first to test it for your next event and I'd love your honest feedback on what sucks.

Also building a creator directory at clublesscollective.com/creators — happy to feature your next show if you want a profile up there.

No rush, no pitch — just tell me if it's interesting or not. 🙏
