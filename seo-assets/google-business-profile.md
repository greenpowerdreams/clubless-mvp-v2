## FAQ Section (add under "Questions & Answers")

**Q: Do I need to own a venue to host a Clubless event?**
A: No. Clubless is built specifically for creators who don't own a venue. We help you book rooms, manage ticketing, and bring in a licensed mobile bar.

**Q: What are your fees?**
A: 8-10% platform fee per ticket sold. No monthly subscription, no upfront cost. You keep the rest.

**Q: Is your bar service licensed?**
A: Yes. Fully licensed through Washington State Liquor & Cannabis Board (WSLCB) and insured.

**Q: What cities do you serve?**
A: Primarily Seattle and the Puget Sound region — Bellevue, Kirkland, Redmond, Tacoma, Everett. We can travel for larger events.

**Q: How is this different from Posh or Eventbrite?**
A: Clubless is built for venue-free creators. We bundle ticketing with mobile bar + vendor tools so you can run an event end-to-end without chasing 5 different providers. We also take less (8-10% vs 10-20%+).
