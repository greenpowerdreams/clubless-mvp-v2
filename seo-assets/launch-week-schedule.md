# Launch Week Schedule — Clubless Collective

**Goal:** Coordinated launch week to maximize visibility, backlinks, and first-page SERP coverage for "clubless" and "clubless collective".

---

## Pre-Launch (Sunday before)

- [ ] Confirm production deploy is live and stable
- [ ] Verify Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster, Plausible all showing data
- [ ] Confirm Google Business Profile is live and approved
- [ ] All social handles live with updated bios linking to clublesscollective.com
- [ ] Draft + schedule IG Reel for Thursday launch
- [ ] Line up 3-5 friends to upvote PH launch in the first hour

## Monday — Product Hunt Launch

**Launch time:** 12:01 AM PT Monday (when PH day starts)
- [ ] Submit to Product Hunt using `product-hunt-launch.md` copy

- [ ] Post launch announcement in Slack/Discord communities you're in
- [ ] 3pm PT: Post mid-day update comment on the PH page
- [ ] Target: Top 5 Product of the Day

## Tuesday — Reddit

- [ ] r/Seattle: post using `reddit-launch-seattle.md` (morning, ~9am)

- [ ] Respond to every comment within 1 hour during first 6 hours

## Wednesday — Hacker News

- [ ] Submit "Show HN:" at 8-10am PT (hn peak time)
