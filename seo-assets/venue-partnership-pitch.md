# Venue Partnership Pitch — Cold Email Template

**Goal:** Recruit 5-10 non-traditional Seattle spaces (warehouses, galleries, cafes, lofts, rooftops) as Clubless partner venues. Each one = a recurring revenue channel AND a likely backlink from their website/IG bio.

**Key reframe:** We're not asking them to be a club. We're asking them to be a canvas — we handle licensing, bar, ticketing, promoter matchmaking. They get off-hours revenue with zero liability.

---
