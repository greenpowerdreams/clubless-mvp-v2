## Subject Line

Local founder launches free tool for Capitol Hill DJs and event creators

## Email Body

Hi CHS team,

I'm Drew Green, a Seattle founder who just launched Clubless Collective — a free platform for event creators, DJs, and promoters who don't own a venue. I'm writing because Capitol Hill is where this idea came from and where most of its early users are.

Over the last two years a lot of the neighborhood's best small-room events have happened outside traditional clubs — in lofts, cafes after hours, galleries, pop-ups. The people running those events have had to DIY everything: ticketing, bar licensing, promoting, vendor booking. Clubless bundles it all into one platform:

- 8-10% ticketing (lower than Posh, Eventbrite, Dice)
- Licensed mobile bar service (we bring it to any space, WSLCB-compliant)
- Vendor marketplace for DJs, photographers, rentals
- Free profit calculator
- Curated Seattle event calendar

It's live at clublesscollective.com and is free to sign up, free to list events, and free to use the calculator.

I think this fits CHS because:

1. It's a Capitol Hill story — built by a local, for the scene that already exists on this hill
2. It speaks to the ongoing question of how nightlife survives rising rents and venue consolidation
3. It might genuinely help some of the creators your readers already follow

I'd be glad to chat, share the founder story, or put you in touch with a few of the Capitol Hill DJs who've been using it. I can also share our first-month metrics once we have them.

Thanks for everything CHS does for this neighborhood.
