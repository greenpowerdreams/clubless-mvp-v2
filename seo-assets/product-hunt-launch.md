## First Maker Comment (post immediately after launch)

Hey Product Hunt 👋

I'm Drew, founder of Clubless Collective. I spent the last two years watching friends in Seattle's nightlife scene get squeezed from both sides — venues taking 20% of the bar plus a room fee, ticketing platforms taking another 10-20%, and DJs splitting what's left four ways.

So I built the tool I wished existed. Clubless is what happens when you unbundle the nightclub:
- **Ticketing** at 8-10% (lower than Posh, Eventbrite, Dice)
- **Licensed mobile bar service** — we bring the bar to any space
- **Vendor marketplace** — DJs, photographers, rentals, all vetted
- **Profit calculator** — know your margin before you book
- **Instant payouts** via Stripe Connect

It's free to sign up, free to list events, and free to use the calculator. We only charge when you sell tickets.

Why "Clubless"? Because the best rooms in Seattle right now aren't clubs — they're warehouses, rooftops, cafes after hours, private lofts. Creators don't need a venue. They need tools.

Would love your feedback — especially from anyone who's ever thrown an event and felt the squeeze. Happy to answer anything.

🍹 Drew

## Mid-day Update Comment (3pm PT)

Quick mid-day update: we're at #X on the leaderboard 🙏 thanks to everyone who upvoted and commented. A few questions I've been getting a lot:

**"Is this only Seattle?"** — For now, yes. We're being deliberate about launching deep before we go wide. Seattle is our home and we want to prove the model here before we expand. If you're in another city and want early access, DM me.

**"How is this different from Posh?"** — Posh is great for college-town ticketing. Clubless is for creators who want more than ticketing — they want the bar, the vendors, the calculator, the whole stack. And our fee is lower (8-10% vs 10-20%).

**"Mobile bar — legit?"** — 100%. Fully licensed through Washington State (WSLCB) and insured. Not a hack. Not a gray area.

Keep the questions coming 👇
